Choosing the right realtor
Jim Brone

March 11, 2014 6:40PM

15 comments

Choosing the right realtor is important when it comes to purchasing a house. When a person walks by in a itty bitty suit, with the right shoes, you get the feeling like maybe this is going to be the right purchase. Commissions are important as well. No one wants to the odd man out.

Color

Color is important when choosing a house, no one wants to get an ugly color that will not sell when it is time to put it back on the market. What about the color of the bedrooms, it has been scientifically proven that it is more difficult to sleep in a room where the color was poorly chosen.

Neighbors

If there is one thing you are going to remember, be sure that it is the neighbors will be a very important discussion topic for you and your loved one. They will be helpful at times, and they will be troublesome at times. I find that a careful balance of freedom is best. Not too much, but just enough if you need a favor.
